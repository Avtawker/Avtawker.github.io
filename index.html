Quark Simulation: Forming Mesons and Baryons

Instructions
  • Click any of the quark or antiquark buttons to generate a quark.
  • Drag and drop the quarks within the simulation area.
  • When two quarks (or particles) are close enough, a card will pop up asking if you want to merge them.
  • If the merged combination forms a known meson or baryon, it will display the particle's name along with its full quark composition.
  • If the combination is not recognized, it will be labeled as "unknown."
  • You can merge up to 5 quarks per particle and have up to 5 particles on the board.

Sparkles Info

The sparkles represent neutrinos – elusive particles that rarely interact with matter. They symbolize the high-energy, dynamic nature of particle physics.
